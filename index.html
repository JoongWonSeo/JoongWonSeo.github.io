Random: 24 Solver in Haskell

24 is a drinking game for nerd parties where you have to come up with a mathematical expression that evaluates to 24 using exactly the numbers on the 4 randomly drawn cards.
For example, if you draw 3, 5, 6 and 9: (6 - 3) * 5 + 9 = 24
I made a Haskell script that solves the game for you. It's not very efficient though, so it might take a while to find a solution. But hey, at least you can get drunk faster.
